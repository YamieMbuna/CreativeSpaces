Legal Policies & Service Commitments

Our commitment to compliance, security, and service reliability, specifically for our Creative Chats platform.

Creative Chats Data Protection Policy

Compliant with Malawi Electronic Transactions & Cybersecurity Act (2023).

1. Data Collection Scope

  • We collect the business WhatsApp number and basic analytics (e.g., message volume, peak times).
  • Anonymized chat logs are stored, subject to user consent
  • Optional end-to-end encryption is available for sensitive data like patient IDs or loan applications.

2. Security Measures

  • Technical Safeguards: Include AES-256 encryption for stored data, monthly penetration testing, and two-factor authentication for admin access.
  • Human Safeguards: Staff undergo data privacy training and sign NDAs, with strict role-based access.
  • Geographical Control: Primary servers are located in South Africa (AWS Cape Town), with an on-premise backup option in Malawi available for an extra fee.

3.Client & End-User Rights

  • User Consent: Chatbots must include the message: "By chatting, you agree to our [Privacy Policy link]. Reply STOP to opt-out."[cite: 20, 21, 22].
  • Breach Protocol: We notify you within **24 hours** of detecting any breach.
  • Access Requests: Customers can request chat logs deletion within **72 hours**.
  • Data Protection Officer: The accountability is managed by our Data Protection Officer.

Service Level Agreement (SLA) for Creative Spaces

Our commitment to uptime and support for WhatsApp chatbot hosting and maintenance.

Service Availability

  • Uptime Guarantee: 98% per month[cite: 50].
  • Maintenance Window: Every last Sunday night from 10 PM to midnight (Central Africa Time)[cite: 51].
  • Service Credits: Available if uptime targets are not met (ranging from 10% to 50% credit on the next invoice).

Support Response Times (Mon-Fri, 7AM to 4PM CAT)

  • Critical Issues (Bot down): Response within **1 hour**; Target resolution within 4 hours.
  • Functional Errors (Bot glitching): Response within 4 business hours; Target resolution within 2 business days.
  • Minor Queries/Tweaks: Response within 1 business day; Resolution within 5 business days.
  • After-Hours Support: Available for critical issues only (additional charges may apply).
